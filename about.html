Agromet Advisory Services.

Our team of agricultural experts provides customized support for every stage of the farming process.Major district level and block level Agromet Advisory Bulletins of the project are being uploaded every Tuesday and Friday on IMD site http://agromet.imd.gov.in and http://www.imdagrimet.gov.in .

Weather Update

District level weather forecast for next 5 days with respect to rainfall, maximum temperature, minimum temperature, wind speed, wind direction, relative humidity and cloudiness, weekly and crop specific advisories is provided to the farmers.

Feedback

Feedback of farmers is also collected through telephone and WhatsApp messages regarding profit/loss incurred through weather based agriculture advisory service bulletin.
